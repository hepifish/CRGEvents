ROXANA CHEREGI

I’ve organized my first event about 25 years ago. I didn’t knew much back then, but what I remember for sure is the fact that I was very passionate and excited about it.

20 years ago I was 10 years old, not enough to have this responsibility, but enough to allow myself to dream about it.

My first real event was at the age of 23, when I had to organize a Charity Prom for the organization were I was working (100 participants).

Along the years, I’ve organized more than 250 events, with 50 to 1000 (and more) guests.

Just to make an idea, in 2018 alone I had 23 events (Corporate Events and Business Conferences to Weddings and Baptism Parties).

If you want to find out more about my experience and how I bring a drop of passion to the events, just write me a note and I hope that that will be the first step of our collaboration.
